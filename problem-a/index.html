Shawn Spencer

Shawn Spencer is a fictional character in the hit show Psych. He co-owns a psychic detective agency with his childhood best friend, Burton Guster. Despite not having any psychic abilities, Shawn poses as a psychic due to his powers of observation. He employs this skill to assist the Santa Barbara Police Department in solving various cases.
